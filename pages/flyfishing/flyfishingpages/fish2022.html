2022 Fishing Report

I'm not going to lie, I didn't do very much fishing in 2022. I went on another road trip to Montana, although most of our time there was spent climbing. It was this year that my fishing momentum really slowed down. I had begun climbing and that took a large majority of my time. I forget what river this was on but I was able to whip up a couple of cutthroat and some whitefish. You can tell that cimbing really took over given by the fact that I can't remember the name of the river. Oh well.

I did make it out to the Yuba too. On this trip I caught one of my biggest trout ever. I remember freaking out as he tore downstream, given that I was fishing with 5x line. My friend Eli ran downstream to net it once he saw the size of it. It was caught on a top secret fly Eli has been tying. After this fish, I hung the fly on my wall and haven't used it since. I haven't really fished the Yuba either since. We have tried once or twice and got skunked. Getting skunked is rough after catching a fish like this one.

I did of course hit Putah Creek a couple of times as well. We caught some pretty good fish there as normal. I dont have any of those on here though. You can check the 2021 fly fishing page though if you want to see some big Putah Creek fish.

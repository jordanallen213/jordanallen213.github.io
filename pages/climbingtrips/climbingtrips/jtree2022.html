Joshua Tree

December 26-31st, 2022

The first part of this trip was a blur. I was working as a line cook at the time and had just gotten off a 10 hour shift. 6am to 4pm. I drove home, loaded all my gear into the car, then hit the road south. My Subaru overheated before I even left town and I had to stop by Target to get more coolant and whatnot. I remember about 8 hours into the drive I was popping Excedrin to stay awake. I made it to J tree eventually.

I'm not sure exactly where i slept that night but i'll tell you that wherever it was, it probably wasn't legal. That's alright though, because I woke up at sunrise to go site hunting in hidden valley. Luckily, some people were on their way out and I was able to snag their spot.

Not much more can be said for this trip. I had some homies pull up and we spent the next few days climbing and hanging out. We weren't extra motivated and only climbed a few pitches a day but it was still a blast.

I remember texting my boss off my garmin inreach that i wasn't coming into work.

We made it up into the space station to play some guitar and hangout. 

It's always pretty fun in the hidden valley campground, I'm just bummed I missed the NYE party.

Welcome to the blog! This is mainly just a way to document what I've been up to. I'll probably just be posting lots of photos and trip reports for friends and family to look at. If you have any comments or questions about the blog or the trips please let me know!

Summer is over (or so I thought)! I just returned to California from Alaska and the heat started all over again! 100 degrees- WTF! Anyway, Im heading up to Oregon in a few days and then out to Wyoming, where I'll be until Winter. Come winter I'll be skiing a ton (I hope) and most likely will be in the Tahoe area.
